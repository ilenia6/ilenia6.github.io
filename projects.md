### 🧬 Transcriptomics Data Analysis

During my PhD, I designed and optimized multi-species transcriptomic workflows for *de novo* assembly, annotation, and differential expression profiling of non-model organisms. Pipelines were built for reproducibility, modularity, and scalability on HPC clusters.

**Tools:** Python, R, Snakemake, Docker, Singularity, Conda, SLURM, BUSCO, OrthoFinder, InterProScan, BLAST, SAMtools, minimap2, BWA, Bowtie  
**Highlights:** comparative transcriptomics and functional annotation published in *Scientific Reports* and *PLOS One*.

🔗 [bioinfo-utils](https://github.com/ilenia6/bioinfo-utils) — Python utilities developed in this context (transcriptome filtering, GFF restructuring, parallel data processing)

### 🗂️ KrillDB2 — Database Reimplementation

Reimplemented and extended *KrillDB2*, a reference database for Antarctic krill transcriptome analysis. Improved data accessibility, added expression visualization tools, and optimized the underlying pipeline for reproducibility and faster updates.

**Contribution:** pipeline reorganization, data integration, cross-dataset normalization  
**Reference:** Urso, I. et al. (2022) *Scientific Reports*. [DOI](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-022-10948-x)

### 🔍 Genome Assembly & Workflow Optimization

Designed and calibrated high-throughput pipelines for genome assembly from third-generation sequencing data (ONT, PacBio) and variant calling within the **ERGA Project** (European Reference Genome Atlas). Focused on workflow automation, resource optimization on HTCondor/SLURM, and reproducibility across distributed analysis sites.

Also contributed to the reconstruction of the nuclear genome and endosymbiont of a zoonotic parasite responsible for ocular onchocerciasis in domestic and wild carnivores and humans.

🔗 [EARs_Bari](https://github.com/ilenia6/EARs_Bari) — ERGA Assembly Reports (QC and validation framework)

### 🔬 Orthologous Group Annotation — orthogen

A Python tool developed during my PhD to automate the integration of OMA (Orthologous Matrix) results with cross-database functional annotations (UniProt, Ensembl, Entrez). Includes preflight data validation, modular processing logic, and test coverage.

**Tools:** Python, Biopython, Pandas  
🔗 [orthogen on GitHub](https://github.com/ilenia6/orthogen)
