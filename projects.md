### 🧬 Transcriptomics Data Analysis
During my PhD, I optimezed multi‑species transcriptomic workflows for *de novo* assembly, annotation, and expression profiling of non‑model organisms.  
The pipelines were designed to ensure high reproducibility, modularity, and automation on HPC clusters.  
**Tools:** R, Python, SLURM, Docker, Singularity, Conda, SNakemake, BUSCO, OrthoFinder, InterProScan, BLAST, samtools, minimap2, bowtie, bwa, genome assemblers  
**Highlights:** comparative transcriptomics and functional annotation published in *Scientific Reports* and *PLOS One*.

### 🗂️ KrillDB2 Database Reimplementation
Reimplemented and improved the *KrillDB2* platform, a database dedicated to krill transcriptome analysis.  
Enhanced data accessibility, added new expression visualization tools, and optimized the underlying pipeline for faster updates.  
**Contribution:** pipeline reorganization, data integration, reproducibility.  
**Reference:** Urso, I. et al. (2022) *Scientific Reports*.

### 🔍 Genome Workflow Optimization
Supported the development and calibration of high‑throughput pipelines for genome assembly, starting from third-generation sequencind data (ONT, PacBio), and variant calling non‑model organisms.  
Contributed to the **ERGA Project**, focusing on workflow automation and HPC resource optimization (HTCondor).  
Additionally, collaborated on the reconstruction of the **nuclear genome and its endosymbiont** from a zoonotic parasite, causes ocular onchocerciasis in both domestic and wild carnivores, as well as humans.
**Outcome:** improved throughput and reproducibility across analysis sites.
