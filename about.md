I'm **Ilenia**, a computational biologist and data scientist passionate about bridging scientific discovery and applied data analysis. With a background in genomics, transcriptomics, and workflow engineering, my work focuses on turning complex data into clear, reproducible, and actionable results.

Over the years, I've worked at the **University of Padova**, the **National Research Council (CNR-IBIOM)**, and the **University of Bari**, contributing to genome assembly, variant calling, and transcriptomic analyses within international consortia such as **ERGA** (European Reference Genome Atlas) and the **Horizon Europe GDI Project**.

I combine an academic researcher's rigor with a data scientist's pragmatic mindset — using automation and reproducibility as tools to create value and impact beyond the lab.
